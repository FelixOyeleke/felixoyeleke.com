This page explains what cookies are, which cookies this site uses, and how you can control them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. They are widely used to keep sites working reliably and remember preferences.

Cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary cookies — These may be set by our hosting provider (Netlify) to deliver the site securely and reliably.
  • No tracking/advertising cookies — We do not use analytics, advertising, or cross-site tracking cookies.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings, including blocking or deleting them. Refer to your browser’s help for instructions. Blocking all cookies may affect how some websites function.

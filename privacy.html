Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 Nov 2025

This website respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Contact form submissions: your name, email address, company (optional), and message.
  • Newsletter form submissions: your email address.
  • Basic system logs from our hosting provider (Netlify), such as IP address and request metadata, for security and reliability.

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries that you submit via the contact form.
  • To send occasional updates if you opt in via the newsletter form.
  • To operate, protect, and improve the site (e.g., preventing abuse and diagnosing issues).

We do not sell your personal data. Information you submit through forms is stored in Netlify’s secure Forms service and delivered to us for follow‑up.

Cookies

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Our hosting provider (Netlify) may set essential, strictly‑necessary cookies to provide the service. For more details, see the Cookie Policy.

Data retention

Form submissions are retained as long as needed to handle your request and maintain records. You can request deletion of your submission at any time.

Your choices and rights

  • You can request access to, or deletion of, your form submission.
  • You may unsubscribe from newsletters at any time using the link provided in messages (if enabled).
  • You can control cookies via your browser settings. Blocking all cookies may affect site functionality.
